Why a one-time "are you sure?" on Windows/macOS?
Proxion ships free and unsigned-by-a-vendor on purpose — no Apple or Microsoft gatekeeping, no certificate fees, no middleman. Updates are still cryptographically verified with Proxion's own signing key. The OS just shows a caution prompt the first time because it can't phone home to a paid certificate authority. Linux has no prompt.
